We are

Aurajoki Overflow is a tech meetup focused on all of the nooks and crannies of working in software.

We organize events and workshops for software professionals

Meetups

Aurajoki Overflow meetups are a monthly events where we get together and discuss a common topic

Topics

Each meetup will have 2-3 presentation on varying topics from Data sciences to Continuous delivery to code editors

Not just presentations

The meetups are the perfect place to find likeminded peers and maybe even hold a lightning talk

Hosting an event

For companies, hosting the meetup works as a perfect opportunity to get dozens of professionals to your offices and show them that you care about the local tech culture.

The only thing you will be in charge of is providing the meetup a proper space: A space with a presentation setup/tv and room for the audience, as well as some food and drinks for the attendees to enjoy while they learn. That's it. We will handle the rest: The marketing, registrations, speakers etc.

Of course! Aurajoki Overflow is for anyone interested in tech.
